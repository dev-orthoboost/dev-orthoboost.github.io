Q.01How much does treatment actually cost?+

It depends on your case, which is why we never quote from a phone call. You get your exact all-in price in writing at your first visit. Simple crowding and relapse cases fit the Quick 6 Fix at $4,299 flat, and every plan can be paid at $1,000 down and $220 a month at 0% interest.

Q.02Why age seven for a first check-up?+

By seven, enough adult teeth are in for a specialist to spot problems early, and often keep them small. Most kids don't need treatment yet; we monitor growth for free until the timing is exactly right.

Q.03Braces or Invisalign — which is right for me?+

Both move teeth well when they're planned well. The honest answer depends on your bite, your habits and your life, which is exactly what the free consultation is for. Dr. Daher will show you both options against your own scan, with a price for each.

Q.04Do you take insurance?+

We work with most extended health plans and handle the paperwork with you at your first visit, so your written price already reflects what your coverage contributes.

Q.05How long will treatment take?+

Most cases run 12–18 months, with visits only every 6–8 weeks. You'll see the full timeline, mapped month by month, before you commit to anything.
