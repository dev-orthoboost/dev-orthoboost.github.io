Common questions

Ask Dr. Williams a question
Am I too old for braces?
Not at all. Teeth can be straightened at just about any age, and adults make up a growing share of the smiles we care for. At your consultation we will look at your teeth and share honest, straightforward options.
Which discreet option is right for me?
It depends on your smile, your goals, and your daily routine. We will help you choose at your free consult, weighing clear braces and Invisalign together so you land on the option that fits you best.
How long does treatment take?
It varies by smile, since every case is a little different. At your consultation we will give you a clear, honest estimate based on what we see, so you know what to expect before anything begins.
