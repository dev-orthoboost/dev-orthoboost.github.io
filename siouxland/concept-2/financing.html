Common questions

Ask Dr. Williams a question
Do you take my insurance?
We accept most insurance plans and are happy to check yours before you commit to anything. Bring your card to your free consultation and we will review your benefits with you and explain what they cover.
What if I don't have orthodontic coverage?
That is very common, and it is not a problem. At your consultation we will walk you through flexible monthly payment options so you can move forward with a plan that fits your family's budget.
