Clear Braces

All the dependable results of traditional braces, with brackets that blend right in. If you or your teen feel a little self-conscious about metal, clear braces are worth a look.

A more discreet way to straighten

Clear braces use tooth-colored ceramic brackets that blend in with your smile, so they are far less noticeable than metal. They work the same proven way, gently guiding your teeth into place, and they give you the same dependable results.

They are a favorite with teens and adults who want to feel comfortable in photos, at school, and at work while they straighten their smile. As always, Dr. Williams plans and monitors every case personally.

Who clear braces are for

  • Teens and adults who feel self-conscious about metal
  • Anyone who wants a discreet, natural-looking option
  • Crowding, spacing, and bite issues of most kinds
  • People who prefer fixed braces over removable aligners

Common Questions

Are clear braces as effective as metal?
Yes. Clear braces move your teeth the same proven way and give the same dependable results. The main difference is the look, since the ceramic brackets blend in with your smile.
Do the clear brackets stain?
The brackets themselves resist staining, and with good brushing and simple daily care they tend to stay looking clean. At your visits we will share easy tips to keep everything bright.
How long does treatment take?
It varies by smile, since every case is a little different. At your consultation we will give you a clear, honest estimate based on what we see, so you know what to expect before anything begins.
