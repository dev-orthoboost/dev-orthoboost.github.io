How It Works

Three simple steps from your first hello to a plan that fits your budget.

Step 1

Free consult and a clear plan

Meet Dr. Williams, get a full look at your smile, and leave with a clear treatment plan and a written breakdown you can take home.

Step 2

We review your insurance and options

We check your benefits for you, explain what they cover in plain language, and lay out the options so nothing is confusing.

Step 3

A monthly plan that fits

Together we choose flexible monthly payment options that work for your family, and then it is time to get started.

Common Questions

Do you take my insurance?
We accept most insurance plans and are happy to check yours before you commit to anything. Bring your card to your free consultation and we will review your benefits with you and explain what they cover.
What if I don't have orthodontic coverage?
That is very common, and it is not a problem. At your consultation we will walk you through flexible monthly payment options so you can move forward with a plan that fits your family's budget.
