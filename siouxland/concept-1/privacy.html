Privacy Policy

This policy explains what we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Siouxland Orthodontics handles the information collected through this website. It covers the details you send us when you fill out a form or contact us, along with basic technical information your browser shares when you visit.

Health information you share with us as a patient is handled separately and is protected under federal law. For that, please see our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices, which describes how we use and disclose your protected health information.

This website is provided for general information only and is not medical advice.

Information We Collect

When you use our contact or consultation form, we collect the information you choose to give us, which typically includes:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • Your preferred office
  • What you are interested in (for example, braces, aligners, or airway-focused care)
  • Any message or notes you add

We also collect basic technical and analytics data automatically, such as your general location, the type of device and browser you use, the pages you view, and how you arrived at our site. This helps us understand how the site is used.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your questions and requests
  • Schedule your consultation
  • Follow up with you about your care and appointments
  • Improve the content and performance of our website

We use a lead-management platform and analytics tools that process this data on our behalf so we can respond to you and keep the site running smoothly.

How We Share It

We do not sell your information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate, such as our lead-management platform, analytics providers, and hosting services, and only so they can perform work for us. We may also share information when required by law or to protect the safety and rights of our patients, staff, or the public.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar tools to remember your preferences and to measure how the site is used. Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings. Turning cookies off may affect how some parts of the site work.

Your Choices

You can choose not to contact us or submit a form. If you have shared information with us, you may ask us to access, update, or delete it where applicable. You can also ask us to stop contacting you at any time. To make a request, reach us using the contact details below.

Children's Privacy

This website is directed to parents and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children online. If you believe a child has provided us information through this site, please contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the date at the top of this page. Please check back so you stay informed about how we handle your information.
