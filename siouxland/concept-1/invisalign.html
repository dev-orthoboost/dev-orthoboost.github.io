Invisalign Clear Aligners

Straighten your teeth with a series of clear, removable trays that most people will never notice. A great fit for teens and busy adults who want to keep life moving.

Straightening that fits your day

Invisalign uses a set of removable, virtually invisible aligners to straighten your teeth without brackets or wires. You wear each set for a couple of weeks, then move to the next, and your smile shifts into place a little at a time.

Because they are removable, you take them out to eat, brush, and enjoy special occasions, then pop them back in. Invisalign is popular with teens and busy adults, and Dr. Williams is an experienced provider who guides your progress at every step.

Why people love aligners

  • Virtually invisible
  • Removable
  • Fewer disruptions to daily life
  • Digital scans, no goopy impressions

Common Questions

How many hours a day do I wear them?
Aligners work best when you wear them most of the day and night, taking them out mainly to eat and brush. We will give you clear guidance at your visit, and the routine becomes second nature quickly.
Can teens use Invisalign?
Many teens do very well with aligners, especially those who are ready to keep up with wearing them. At your consultation Dr. Williams will help you decide whether aligners or braces are the better fit.
How long does treatment take?
It varies by smile, since every case is a little different. At your consultation we will give you a clear, honest estimate based on what we see, so you know what to expect before anything begins.
