Metal Braces

Time-tested, dependable, and a little more comfortable than they used to be. If your child needs braces, or you have been thinking about your own smile, we will walk you through every step.

What metal braces do

Metal braces are the proven, reliable way to correct crowding, spacing, and bite issues. Small brackets and a gentle wire guide your teeth into place over time, and today's braces are smaller and more comfortable than the ones you might remember.

Dr. Williams places and monitors every case personally, so the plan stays on track and you always know what comes next. Braces work beautifully for kids, teens, and adults alike.

Who metal braces are for

  • Kids and teens who are ready to start treatment
  • Adults who want a dependable, proven approach
  • Crowding, spacing, and bite issues of most kinds
  • Families who want a straightforward, budget-friendly plan

Common Questions

How long does treatment take?
It varies from smile to smile, since every case is a little different. At your consultation we will give you a clear, honest estimate based on what we see, so you know what to expect before anything begins.
Do braces hurt?
Most people feel some tenderness for a few days after braces go on or after an adjustment, and that usually settles quickly. We will share simple tips to keep you comfortable, and you can always reach out if something does not feel right.
How often are visits?
Most patients come in every several weeks so we can check progress and make small adjustments. We will set a rhythm that fits your calendar, and we offer before and after school appointments to make things easier.
