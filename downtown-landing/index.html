More than half of kids develop a bite issue

About 56% of children and adolescents worldwide have some form of malocclusion. Most still just need watching, which is exactly what a free growth check at age seven is for.

Lombardo et al., European Journal of Paediatric Dentistry, 2020 · systematic review & meta-analysis

Treating prominent front teeth early roughly halves new injuries

Share of kids in clinical trials who suffered a new front-tooth injury. Early treatment for prominent upper front teeth cut new incisal trauma from 37% to 21%.

Batista et al., Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews, 2018 · Class II malocclusion in children

Why the first check happens at seven

An orthodontic check by age seven, while the jaw is still growing. Most visits end in simple monitoring, and early treatment — an expander or partial braces, if anything — is timed for when it genuinely helps.

American Association of Orthodontists · check-up recommendation, no later than age 7

The rest of the picture
Who will actually be treating us?

Dr. Daher, from your first visit to your last. He's a certified specialist in orthodontics with over 30 years in practice, and you'll see the same doctor and the same small team throughout, with no rotating faces and no handoffs.

Braces or Invisalign: how do we choose?

At your consult we'll walk through both, honestly, for your specific smile. Some cases do beautifully with Invisalign; others are better and faster with braces. We charge the same for both, so the recommendation you get is about your bite, not our margin.

How long does treatment usually take?

Most full plans run 12 to 24 months, and simpler express cases considerably less. That's the point of the Quick 6 Fix. You'll get a realistic timeline at your consultation, not a vague guess, and we'll keep you updated as you progress.

What about retainers when we're done?

Every plan finishes with clear retainers and a retention plan, because teeth drift if you let them. Dr. Daher checks your retention himself, and if an old retainer has stopped fitting, we can help with that too.

What if we move partway through treatment?

It happens, and we make it painless. We'll prepare your records and help transfer your care to a trusted orthodontist near your new home, with your treatment plan fully documented.
